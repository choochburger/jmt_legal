Practice Areas

Protecting Your Intellectual Property

http://trademarkyourcompany.com/

The Law Office of John M. Trani understands that your brand is valuable and important to you. We will take the appropriate steps to ensure that your trademarks, trade secrets, copyrights and other intellectual property are protected.

We offer a wide range of intellectual property and brand management services, including: trademark and copyright registration application filing, trademark search and availability assessment, trademark monitoring, responses to trademark office actions, trademark oppositions, licensing agreements, cease and desist letters, non-disclosure agreements, confidentiality agreements, employee invention agreements, website terms of use and privacy policies, domain name dispute resolution and intellectual property litigation.

Helping You Build Your Business

Are you starting a new business? Have you had an offer to buy your business? Or are you already running an established business that needs reorganization? Whatever your business’ needs are, we can help. We work with businesses of all sizes and types including start-up, new media, entertainment and non-profit businesses.

We help you select the appropriate entity type for your business and properly form it with the government. We also draft and negotiate a variety of corporate contracts, including operating agreements, shareholder agreements, stock purchase agreements, member buyouts and company acquisitions and mergers.

Litigating and Settling Disputes

Not all transactions and business relationships go well. If you find yourself in a failed situation, we can help you litigate or settle your dispute. We represent businesses and professionals in intellectual property, business and employment related lawsuits.

The types of lawsuits we handle include trademark infringement/dilution, TTAB litigation, copyright infringement, unfair competition, defamation, violation of privacy, publicity rights, partnership, member and shareholder disputes, breach of contract, tortious interference, default on promissory note/debt, employment discrimination and labor.

Entertainment And Media

The Law Office of John M. Trani represents filmmakers, musicians, writers, photographers, producers and directors as well as media and entertainment related companies in a variety of transactions. These transactions include brand and software licensing, recording agreements, venue agreements, synchronization agreements, management agreements, merchandizing agreements, administrative agreements, talent agreements, production agreements, location agreements, employment agreements, model and property releases.

Helping You Staff

Sometimes the best talent is found outside of our country. We can help you procure visas so you can build your employee base. We offer a wide range of visas, including H1-B, E-1, E-2, E-3, L-1, O-1 and TN-1.

Profile

John M. Trani is the principal of The Law Office of John M. Trani. Mr. Trani focuses his practice on intellectual property and brand management, business and entertainment transactions, immigration matters and general litigation. In addition, Mr. Trani is the General Counsel of Carrot Creative, LLC, a digital and new-media marketing agency focusing on social media.

Before founding his own law firm, Mr. Trani was an associate at Ross & Asmar LLC, a general practice. During his time at Ross & Asmar LLC, Mr. Trani litigated a variety of cases and counseled on transactions involving intellectual property, business, immigration and labor and employment matters.

He also served as a law clerk at the Federal Department of Labor and for the Honorable William Macauley at the Federal Equal Employment Opportunity Commission.

Moreover, he has worked on various corporate, intellectual property and entertainment transactions in the legal departments of DC Comics and Blitz Distribution, Inc., a leading manufacturer of skateboard equipment and apparel, and at multiple entertainment law firms, including Carroll, Guido & Groffman LLP.

Mr. Trani earned his law degree from Brooklyn Law School. Prior to law school, Mr. Trani graduated from the Boston University School of Management, where he earned a B.S. in business administration with a concentration in finance. Mr. Trani also completed Dean's List coursework at Purdue University, Villanova University, and Southwestern Law School.

Mr. Trani is admitted to practice before the federal and state courts of New York. Mr. Trani is a member of the New York State Bar Association, New York County Lawyers’ Association and the Brooklyn Bar Association.
